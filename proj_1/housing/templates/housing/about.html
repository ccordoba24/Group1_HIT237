About the NT Housing Maintenance System

What is this system?

The NT Housing Maintenance System is a digital platform designed to help tenants in Northern Territory remote communities report housing repair and maintenance issues, and to allow NT Housing staff and authorized administrators to track, update, and manage those requests efficiently in one place.

Our Purpose

This system provides transparency and efficiency in managing repairs across remote communities in the Northern Territory. It enables:

  • Easy reporting: Tenants can log repair requests quickly and easily from any location
  • Real-time tracking: Tenants can see the progress of their repairs at any time
  • Better coordination: Staff can manage requests and schedule repairs more effectively
  • Complete documentation: A full maintenance history is maintained for each dwelling
  • Improved response: Priority repairs are identified and handled faster

Who We Serve

This system is for NT Housing tenants living in remote communities throughout the Northern Territory. Whether you need urgent repairs due to a safety issue or routine maintenance like plumbing, electrical, air conditioning, or general upkeep, this system provides a simple way to request help.

What You Can Do

Through this system, you can:

  • Submit repair requests for housing maintenance issues
  • Track the status of your repairs in real-time
  • View the complete maintenance history for your dwelling
  • Edit your repair request details (before work begins)
  • Access information about repair timelines and processes
  • Report emergency issues immediately

Key Features

  • Repair Requests: Submit new repair requests and track their status through each stage (Pending, In Progress, On Hold, Completed)
  • Maintenance History: View all completed repairs and maintenance work on your dwelling
  • Status Updates: Receive detailed updates as tradespeople work on your repairs
  • Priority Handling: Emergency and urgent repairs are prioritized for faster response
  • Secure Access: Your housing information is protected with secure login credentials

Designed for Remote Communities

The NT Housing Maintenance System is specifically designed to work effectively in remote Northern Territory communities. We understand the unique challenges of remote living, including:

  • Limited availability of local tradespeople
  • Varying internet connectivity
  • Greater distances between communities
  • Weather and seasonal impacts on repairs
Our system is built to handle these challenges and ensure that tenants in remote areas receive the maintenance support they need.

No Cost to Tenants

Repairs covered by NT Housing tenancy agreements are provided at no cost to you. This system tracks maintenance requests and work—no payment or billing occurs through this app. Repairs required due to normal wear and tear are the responsibility of NT Housing.
