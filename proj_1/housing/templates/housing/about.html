About the NT Housing Maintenance System

A digital platform for tenants in Northern Territory remote communities to report housing repairs, and for NT Housing staff to track and manage those requests.

What is this system?

The NT Housing Maintenance System helps tenants report housing repair and maintenance issues and allows NT Housing staff and administrators to track, update, and manage those requests in one place.

Our Purpose

This system provides transparency and efficiency in managing repairs across remote communities. It enables:

  • Easy reporting: Tenants can log repair requests quickly from any location
  • Real-time tracking: Tenants can see the progress of their repairs at any time
  • Better coordination: Staff can manage requests and schedule repairs more effectively
  • Complete documentation: A full maintenance history is maintained for each dwelling
  • Improved response: Priority repairs are identified and handled faster

Who We Serve

This system is for NT Housing tenants living in remote communities throughout the Northern Territory. Whether you need urgent repairs or routine maintenance, this system provides a simple way to request help.

What You Can Do

  • Submit repair requests for housing maintenance issues
  • Track the status of your repairs in real-time
  • View the complete maintenance history for your dwelling
  • Edit your repair request details (before work begins)
  • Access information about repair timelines and processes
  • Report emergency issues immediately

Key Features

  • Repair Requests: Submit and track requests through each stage
  • Maintenance History: View all completed repairs for your dwelling
  • Status Updates: Receive detailed updates as work progresses
  • Priority Handling: Emergency repairs are prioritized
  • Secure Access: Your housing information is protected with secure login

Designed for Remote Communities

Built to work effectively in remote Northern Territory communities, including:

  • Limited availability of local tradespeople
  • Varying internet connectivity
  • Greater distances between communities
  • Weather and seasonal impacts on repairs

No Cost to Tenants

Repairs covered by NT Housing tenancy agreements are provided at no cost to you. Payment and billing are not managed through this app.
