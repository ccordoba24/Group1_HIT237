Frequently Asked Questions

Below are common questions and answers about the NT Housing Maintenance System.

What is this system used for?

This system helps tenants report housing repair issues and allows staff or admin users to track, update, and manage those requests in one place.

Who can submit a repair request?

Logged-in users can submit repair requests for housing issues such as plumbing, doors, air conditioning, and other maintenance problems.

Can tenants see the progress of their repair request?

Yes. Tenants can view the status of their own repair requests, such as pending, in progress, or completed.

Can tenants update the repair status?

No. Tenants can view the status, but only staff or admin users can change the official repair status.

Can tenants add maintenance updates?

No. Maintenance updates are added by staff or admin users so the repair history stays accurate and reliable.

Can tenants edit their own request?

Yes. Tenants can edit basic request details, but they cannot change the repair status.

How can I reset my password?

Password reset is not available in this version of the system. Users should contact the system administrator if they need account help.

Do tenants pay anything when a tradesperson comes to the home?

No. This system is designed for housing maintenance tracking. Payment handling is not managed by tenants in the app.

How long will I wait for the repair to be completed?

Repair time can vary depending on urgency, staff availability, and the type of maintenance issue. Urgent issues should be handled faster than standard repairs.
