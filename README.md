# Project Brief 

## Theme 3. Remote Housing Crisis centred in NT

We are completing, as our project, an app that helps tackle the current housing crisis in the NT. Currently, about 6000 people are on the public housing waitlist, and many homes face long delays for repairs and recycling for new tenants. We aim to help reduce the stresses and issues seen by many tenants when requesting repairs and thereby speeding up the processes to bring public houses back into the market and help maintain these assets viable for future tenants in the NT. This can help reduce the waiting lists for public housing by having more homes available sooner through current upkeep and quicker repairs.
